Privacy Policy

Color Flip Dash

Last Updated: March, 2026

👋 1. Introduction

Welcome to Color Flip Dash ("we," "our," or "us"), developed by Chamix Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our free mobile game on Android. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to being transparent about our practices.

📊 2. Information We Collect

Color Flip Dash is a free game supported by advertisements. We do not directly collect any personal information from you. However, our advertising partner, Google AdMob, may collect certain information to provide and improve ad services:

Information collected by Google AdMob:

  • Device information (device type, model, operating system version)
  • Advertising ID (used for personalized ads)
  • IP address (approximate location)
  • App usage data (time spent in app, interactions)
  • Ad interaction data (ads viewed, clicked)

🎯 3. How Information is Used

The information collected by Google AdMob is used for the following purposes:

  • Display advertisements: Show relevant ads within the app
  • Personalize ads: Provide ads based on your interests
  • Measure performance: Track ad effectiveness and engagement
  • Improve services: Enhance ad quality and relevance
  • Prevent fraud: Detect and prevent fraudulent ad activity

The game displays three types of ads:

  • Banner ads: Small ads displayed on menu and other non-gameplay screens.
  • Interstitial ads: Full-screen ads that may appear at natural transition points such as between game sessions or when navigating menus.
  • Rewarded ads: Optional ads that players may watch voluntarily in exchange for in-game benefits such as continuing gameplay.

🔗 4. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our app. AdMob may use cookies, device identifiers, and similar technologies to provide personalized advertising experiences.

The app uses the Google AdMob advertising SDK which may collect and process data in accordance with Google's privacy policies.

The app requests the following Android permissions solely to support ad functionality:

  • INTERNET — required to load and display ads
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — checks if a network connection is available before loading ads
  • AD_ID — allows AdMob to access the Android Advertising ID on Android 13+ for ad personalisation

We do not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or storage.

Learn more about Google's privacy practices:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob & Privacy
  • Manage Your Ad Settings

👶 5. Children's Privacy

Color Flip Dash is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action to remove such information.

💾 6. Data Storage

Your game progress, including:

  • High score
  • Settings preferences (music on/off, sound on/off)

Are stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, store, or transmit this information to our servers. This means:

  • ✅ Your data stays on your device
  • ✅ We cannot access your game progress
  • ✅ Uninstalling the app removes all local data

🛡️ 7. Your Rights & Choices

You have control over your privacy. Here's what you can do:

Opt-out of Personalized Ads:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  • Alternative: Settings → Privacy → Ads → "Limit Ad Tracking"

Reset Your Advertising ID:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Reset advertising ID"

Delete App Data:

  • Uninstall the app at any time to remove all locally stored data

🔒 8. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information processed through our app. Notable security measures include:

  • The app enforces HTTPS-only network communication — cleartext (unencrypted) HTTP traffic is blocked
  • Game data saved on your device is protected by Android's standard app sandboxing — other apps cannot access it

However, please be aware that:

  • No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure
  • No electronic storage method is completely secure
  • We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information

🌍 9. International Data Transfers

Google AdMob may transfer and process data in countries other than your own. By using our app, you consent to such transfers in accordance with Google's privacy policies.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
